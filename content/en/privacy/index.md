# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2024**

## Overview

Chelsea Medical Marketing Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

## Information We Collect

When you contact us or use our services, we may collect:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, company name
- **Business Information:** Details about your practice, website, and marketing goals
- **Communication Records:** Correspondence and consultation notes

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Respond to your inquiries and provide our services
- Develop and implement digital marketing strategies
- Communicate about projects, updates, and relevant industry insights
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (analytics, email platforms, project management tools) to deliver our services. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

## Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Request data portability

## Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.
